Privacy Policy

At ScanSave, we take your privacy seriously. Our app is designed with a privacy-first approach, ensuring your data remains secure and under your control at all times.

Privacy-First Design

ScanSave is built with your privacy in mind:

  • Advanced on-device text processing technology
  • Secure local storage for all your scanned content
  • Complete data privacy and control
  • Zero data exposure to external sources

App Permissions

To provide you with the best text scanning experience, we only require:

  • Camera access - enabling our advanced text scanning features

Data Security

Your privacy is protected through our secure design:

  • Advanced encryption for stored data
  • Private on-device processing
  • Secure data management

Your Data Control

You maintain complete control over your data through your device settings. Manage your scanned content directly through the app's interface or your device's storage settings.

Technology

ScanSave utilizes cutting-edge on-device machine learning and text recognition technologies to deliver fast and accurate results while maintaining the highest standards of privacy.

Children's Privacy

While our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age, our privacy-first approach ensures that all users' data remains secure and private on their personal devices.

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app updates available on the Google Play Store.
